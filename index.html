Piano Transcription Onset F-1 Random Gaussian Shift Listening Test

What it is and How to do it

To understand the onset F-1 score, I will randomly generate the number using a zero-mean normal distribution with a different standard deviation (std) and shift the note accordingly for each test. The test contains four different segments from same piece that falls under a specific range of F-1 score. I won't share the F-1 score between the reference section and randomly shifted section to eleminate the bias. One has to listen to the pair of audios and shares thought on whether they sound same. This survey will helps us to find the threshold onset F-1 score which the listener starts to recognize the differences.

Google Form Link

Listen to the pair of audios from the test set, and simply answer yes if they sound similar or no if they are not. You can use link to submit the your answer or email me dhtheuno@gmail.com with "yes" or "no" answer for each segment.
